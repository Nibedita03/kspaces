Project Info — Rajahmundry Flat

A warm, contemporary family residence that pairs clean modern lines with rooted, culturally grounded details — most strikingly a backlit stone Krishna niche framed in a fluted-glass and wood partition at the entry foyer.

The living room makes a confident statement with a sculptural red leather sofa set against wood panelling and expressive feature art, opening into a soft-grey modular kitchen with warm wood uppers and a generous island. Bedrooms range from a classic four-poster with a coral headboard and cane wardrobes to serene, art-led spaces with bespoke murals, a crane-screen headboard and a fully glazed walk-in wardrobe.

Vastu-aligned planning, layered lighting and premium finishes carry through every room — a turnkey home that feels both elegant and effortlessly liveable.
