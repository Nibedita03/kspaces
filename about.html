Where Elegance Finds a Home

K Spaces Interiors blends real estate expertise with inspired interior design — creating spaces that are both beautiful and purposeful. We serve premium flats, luxury villas & commercial environments with tailored solutions that balance aesthetics and practicality, delivering value-engineered transformations you'll love.

Our one-stop design process covers everything from space planning and concept to material selection, execution, and post-handover support. With a dedicated team of experienced designers and the backing of PropGo's real estate knowledge, we deliver smart, timely, and premium-finish interiors.

From stylish 2BHK interiors to luxury villas and dynamic commercial spaces, our portfolio reflects a seamless blend of modern design, functionality, and Vastu-aligned planning — delivered turnkey with premium finishes.

Top Management

RAJESH BHAIRAGOWNI

FOUNDER, PRINCIPAL ARCHITECT

With an academic background in Architecture from SVCA (JNAFAU affiliated) Hyderabad, Rajesh envision himself as a professional in an area where social and environmental responsibility is of utmost importance in both private and public sector. Rajesh’s eye for design detailing and expertise in construction technologies has enabled interpreting the firms vocabulary in new and innovative ways. He believes successful architecture responds to client and user needs concurrently emphasizing on spatial composition and design.

He served as a Sr. Associate Architect at reputed Architectural firms for over 14 years, playing a key role in design and conceptualization and later established K Spaces in 2014.

Specialization: Architect
University: Jawaharlal Nehru architecture and fine arts university
Designation: Founder, Principal Architect
