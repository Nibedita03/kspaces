Our 5-Step Process

From the first conversation to the final styling, our turnkey process keeps things clear, collaborative and on schedule — Vastu-aligned planning and premium finishes throughout.

01

Requirement Gathering

We begin by understanding how you live and work — your needs, lifestyle, taste and budget — so every decision that follows is rooted in you.

02

Concept & 3D Design

Our designers translate the brief into a concept and bring it to life with 3D visualizations and walkthroughs, so you experience your space before it's built.

03

Material Selection & Budgeting

We curate premium, durable finishes and lock a transparent budget and timeline — clear quotes from day one, no hidden costs.

04

Execution & Supervision

Skilled teams execute on site with structured, real-time project tracking and on-ground supervision at every stage.

05

Handover & After-Support

We hand over on time and stay with you — dedicated post-installation care and end-to-end support for lasting quality.
