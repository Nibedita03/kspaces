Project Info — Knack Studios

A creative commercial workspace built around a bold, expressive identity — anchored by the studio's mantra walls “The Change”, “Just Do It” and “Stay True” that greet you the moment you step in.

The design balances raw, industrial textures — micro-cement walls, breeze-block screens and an exposed grid ceiling — against warm wood, brushed-gold columns and layered greenery. From the sculptural reception and breakout games lounge to focused workstations, a private cabin, a conference room and an in-house screening lounge, every zone is tuned for both productivity and personality.

Curated lighting, framed art and a calm outdoor terrace complete a workplace that feels less like an office and more like a studio with a soul.
